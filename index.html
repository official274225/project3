About Us

We are a team of dedicated students from Jigme Namgyel Engineering College, working on a project to create a comprehensive geospatial database system. Our goal is to provide easy access to spatial data for academic and research purposes. This project was inspired by Bhutan NSDI System and it replicates the same system for JNEC.
